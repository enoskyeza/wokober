Mentorship Programme - 2023

The mentorship program nurtures young learners aged 3-17 in creativity, innovation, self-development, and leadership through the Wokober proprietary alternative learning approach, which leverages the uncultivated uniqueness of toy making.

MProgramme Launch.

launched on 15th May, 2023 at Pearl theatre, Elepahnte commons brought together all the 18 learners identified during 2022 Toy Festival, parents and Wokober partners such as GWED-G, Elephante Commons and Wend Africa.

Study tours

Every creation process starts with imagination! Study tours broadens and exposes the minds of the learners to different possibilities beyond what they already know. Wokober partnered with many institutions such as Fundi bots; Wend Africa; Mtindo Designs; Taka-Taka plastics; Sparky Technologies; Daniel Comboni; Oysters and pearls;
