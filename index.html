We strive to nurture Learners with creative ability to their full potential to be creators, problem solvers and leaders, for a better world.

Northern Uganda, especially the Acholi sub-region, recovering from over two decades of conflict, faces a huge education crisis. The current traditional education system falls short of nurturing the human resources with the skill sets, values, and exposure required to meet the needs of this dynamic recovering society. Currently, the region experiences an 80% unemployment rate and a 67% poverty rate, according to UNPA (2022). As Wokober, we believe that for society to emerge from this deep pit, we need a generation of Innovators, Entrepreneurs, and Leaders who are well-exposed, empowered, and anchored in core values, to not only thrive in this dynamic world but, more importantly, to directly participate in uplifting and accelerating the growth of their communities. That's why we are pioneering the Wokober Learning Approach, an alternative creative learning approach that leverages the uncultivated uniqueness of toy-making, aimed at nurturing creativity, innovation, self-development, and leadership.

Featured Project

Toy Making & Innovation Festival 2023

The Wokober Toy & Innovation Festival 2023, was the second of its kind. It was a competitive event, aimed at promoting creativity, innovation and technology exploration within the communities. During the event 30-50 children who exhibit brilliance, potential and passion are identi ed and awarded in three categories (3-7, 8-12 and 13-17 years).

Featured Cause

Mentorship Programme 2023

This is a project based 1 year learning program that starts with study tours to expose the learners to di erent spheres of creativity, global trends in innovation, processes, tools, technology and many others. The study tours is followed by series of personal projects selected by the learners that nurtures imagination, critical thinking, practical exploration, real world application and self-development.

Featured Cause

Pavilion Design Competition 2023

This is a project design competition where pavilion designs are submitted in by different architect individuals or team of indiviuals. The pavillion is an important integral part of the festival experience and winning design is implemented. The team that won the design competition worked with Wokober team and other fabricators to fabricate/build and set up the pavilion.
