Toy Festival-2022

#Wokober Toy Festival - 2022

Toy making as an approach
to creative learning.

The festival was organised as a competitive event under the theme showcasing creative abilities in children for children in three different age groups of 0-8 years, 9-12 years, 13-15 years. It attracted 69 participants, the youngest at 2.5 years old and the oldest at 15 years old.

Winners from the different categories

The toy making festival was successfully organized as a competitive event among children of general age brackets of 4 to 16 years.
