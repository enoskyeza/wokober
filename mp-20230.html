Mentorship Program-2023

Toy making as an approach
to creative learning.

The Learning and Mentorship program builds up from the toy making festival, were selected winners are then trained to further develop their creative skills and knowledge with the vision that they shall be the creators of a better world in the future. It is an 8 months project based program, were learners work on personal projects while being guided by facilitators and mentors through a series of learning modules (inspired from Toy making process i.e. Exposure, Ideation, creation, product application and personal leadership) designed to fully nurture their creative gift.
